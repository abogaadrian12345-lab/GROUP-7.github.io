Executive Director's Message

Welcome to HopeCare Hospital — Uganda's premier health institution dedicated to providing world-class specialized medical care, training, and research. As we continue to grow and modernize our facilities, we remain committed to our core mission of delivering quality and affordable health care services to the people of Uganda and beyond. Together, we are building a healthier nation.


– Prof. Byarugaba Baterana
Executive Director, Mulago National Referral Hospital

Our Vision, Mission & Values

🎯 Mission

To provide specialized health care, training, and research services to the population of Uganda and beyond.

👁️ Vision

To be the leading center of tertiary health care delivery in Africa.

🥅 Goal

To increase access to quality and affordable specialized health care services for the population of Uganda and beyond.

📋 Objectives

Strengthen institutional capacity, improve patient outcomes, advance medical education, and promote research excellence.

Core Values

Health & Safety Professionalism Accountability Integrity Responsiveness Teamwork
