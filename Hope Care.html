Executive Director's Message

Dr. Mark Gideon

HopeCare Hospital is located in Rubaga Hill west of Kampala, the capital city of Uganda. It was founded on 19th December,2006 as a center for treating highly contagious diseases. We offer Surgery and other Internal medicine services and their sub-specialities.

The diagnostic services include Physiotherapy, Pharmatology and many other. Our emergency services include accident and emergency for surgical cases and medical emergency unit for adults and this is operational 24/7.

HopeCare Hospital is grateful to the government of Uganda for providing the necessary resources to run all these services. I also wish to thank our partners both local and international, for their continued support. To the staff of HCH, thank you for the service and let's work harder for better service delivery. Lastly to all our clients, thank you for trusting us to take care of your lives.

OUR VISION AND MISSION

Mission
To provide accessible, high-quality and compassionate primary healthcare services to all individuals.

Vision
To be the trusted healthcare home for our community, where every individual achives their highest potential for health and wellbeing.

Goal
To reduce the prevalence of preventable chronic diseases in our catchment area by 20% over the next three years through proactive community screenings and health education.
