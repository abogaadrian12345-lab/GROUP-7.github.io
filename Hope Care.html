OUR VISION AND MISSION

Mission
To provide accessible, high-quality and compassionate primary healthcare services to all individuals.

Vision
To be the trusted healthcare home for our community, where every individual achives their highest potential for health and wellbeing.

Goal
To reduce the prevalence of preventable chronic diseases in our catchment area by 20% over the next three years through proactive community screenings and health education.
